About Me

Versatile musical theatre student with experience in acting, voice, dance, directing, producing, costume designing, and writing with a desire to gain more experience in directing and producing original works for the stage and screen. Directed, designed, and produced a collegiate production of an original one act at the University of Miami Hecht Studio Theatre. Co-Founded the annual event Canes Care: A Broadway Cares/Equity Fights AIDS Benefit Concert, was heavily involved in the planning process, and is active in supporting the non-profit Broadway Cares/Equity Fights AIDS in her personal life. Prior experience on the Florida 4-H State Executive Board, Florida 4-H Legislative Committee, and involvement in numerous stage productions both on and offstage has provided a proficiency in the International Phonetic Alphabet, public speaking, multitasking, communication, teamwork, creative thinking, and problem solving. Technical proficiencies include WordPress, MS Word, Outlook, PowerPoint, Excel, Adobe Illustrator, InDesign, Photoshop, and various social media platforms such as Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram. Can write, read, speak, and understand Spanish at an intermediate level.
